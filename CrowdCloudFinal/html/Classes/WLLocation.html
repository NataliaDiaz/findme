Overview

The WLLocation class describes the position of the user at a certain time within a Walkbase Analytics-enabled area.

WLLocation objects are delivered via the WLLocationManagerDelegate callback methods. They describe the customer’s position in terms of latitude / longitude and additionally contain a numerical site ID and a floor level. They also contain an estimated horizontalAccuracy which may be utilized if one plans to draw the user’s location on a map for example.
